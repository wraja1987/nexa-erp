# Data Retention Policy — Template

Last updated: 2025-09-04

## Purpose
Define retention periods for Nexa ERP data.

## Who should read this
Ops, security, compliance, and data owners.

## Schedules (editable)
- Operational data: 7 years
- Audit logs: 1 year minimum
- Backups: 30–90 days rolling
- Connector logs: 90 days
- Exports: 30 days unless business need

## Justification
- Legal and accounting obligations
- Security and operational needs

## Review
Review at least annually, or after major changes.
