About Nexa ERP

Nexa ERP is built by Chief A.A Ltd to make enterprise planning simpler, smarter and more secure for every business.

We serve startups, growing companies and large organisations. Our approach is to remove complexity, improve compliance, and give you a system that adapts to the way you work. Nexa brings finance, operations and projects into one place, with Artificial Intelligence assistance where it helps most.

Our story

Chief A.A Ltd is a UK-based company working across technology, consulting, property and investment. Our team has supported businesses across many sectors and sizes. We saw the same problems again and again: systems were expensive, hard to use, and too rigid. Nexa was created to change that.

We created Nexa ERP to give all businesses the tools normally reserved for large enterprises — without the cost and complexity. Whether you are laying foundations, scaling production or managing complex services, Nexa adapts to your needs.

  • Fair monthly pricing and plans that grow with your team.
  • Built-in Artificial Intelligence to cut manual work and surface insight.
  • Compliance and security as standard, with clear audit trails.
  • Flexible modules you can switch on as you need them.

What makes us different

Artificial Intelligence at the core

Insight panels, prompts and automation help your team work faster without losing control. You decide where Artificial Intelligence is used.

Compliance first

Tools for General Data Protection Regulation, audit logging and HM Revenue and Customs Making Tax Digital keep you ready for inspections.

Adaptable to every industry

Finance, projects, retail, distribution, manufacturing — Nexa fits each sector by switching on the modules you need.

Secure by design

Role-based access control, segregation of duties, encryption and full audit history are built into the platform.

Transparent pricing

Plans start from £99 per month. Add users and modules as you grow.
