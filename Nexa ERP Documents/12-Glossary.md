# Glossary

- **KPI** — Key Performance Indicator shown on dashboard/module pages.
- **RBAC** — Role-Based Access Control; access by role and tenant.
- **MTD VAT** — HMRC Making Tax Digital for VAT.
- **DR** — Disaster Recovery; restore within RTO/RPO targets.
- **SLO** — Service Level Objective (latency, uptime).
