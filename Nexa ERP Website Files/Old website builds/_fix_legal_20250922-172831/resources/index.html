Getting Started

Once your subscription package has been chosen, follow these steps to get set up quickly and safely:

  1. Create your company profile — Enter legal name, address, default currency, tax settings and financial year. Set your document numbering (quotes, orders, invoices).
  2. Add users and roles — Invite your team, assign role‑based access control, and enforce two‑factor sign‑in for all users.
  3. Set your financial settings — Build your chart of accounts, tax codes, control accounts and posting rules. Choose bank accounts and starting balances.
  4. Import your data — Load customers, suppliers, products and opening balances using comma separated values templates. Validate results and correct any issues.
  5. Connect payments and banking (optional) — Use Stripe for card payments and TrueLayer for bank feeds. Live keys are required for production.
  6. Configure modules —
    • Finance: sales and purchase invoice workflows, period close, fixed assets and depreciation.
    • Sales and Purchasing: quotation and order types, approvals, price lists and terms.
    • Inventory and Warehouse: warehouses, locations, units of measure, stock posting rules.
    • Projects: templates, task boards, rates and approval steps.
    • Point of Sale (if used): connect Stripe Terminal, set taxes and receipt templates, test a sale.
  7. Compliance and audit — Enable audit trail, set retention rules, confirm General Data Protection Regulation tools, and connect HM Revenue and Customs Value Added Tax (when live keys are available).
  8. Run an end‑to‑end test — Quote → Order → Delivery → Invoice → Payment → Bank reconciliation → Reporting.
  9. Train and go‑live — Walk teams through their daily tasks, check access and approvals, and schedule your go‑live date.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I invite my team?

Go to Settings → Users, select “Invite user”, choose a role and send the invite. We recommend enabling multi‑factor authentication for all users.

FAQ

How do I import my data?

Use the import tools in each module to upload products, customers, suppliers and opening balances. Sample comma separated values templates are provided in each screen.

FAQ

Can I connect payments and bank feeds?

Yes. Stripe handles card payments, and TrueLayer provides bank feeds. Live keys are required for production.

FAQ

How is access controlled?

Use role‑based access control and Segregation of Duties rules to keep responsibilities separate. You can enforce two‑factor sign‑in for all users.

FAQ

Where do I find audit logs?

Each module includes an activity view. You can export logs for your auditors and set retention periods to match your policy.

FAQ

How do I get help?

Please contact us via the Contact page and we will respond as soon as possible, typically within one working day.
