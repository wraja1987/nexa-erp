# How to Use Nexa (Step-by-Step)

This guide explains how to use Nexa in clear, simple steps.

## 1) Sign in
Open the Nexa login page, enter your email and password, then sign in.

## 2) Dashboard
You will see key figures (KPIs), quick links, and recent activity. Use these to jump to your tasks.

## 3) Core areas
- Finance; Sales & CRM; Inventory & WMS; Purchasing; Projects; HR & Payroll; Manufacturing.

## 4) Daily routine
Check KPIs and alerts, action your tasks, and use the sidebar to open modules.

## 5) Getting help
Use the website contact page to reach our team.
